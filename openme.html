A fruitful consideration is: for a process that seeks to maximise replication, what sorts of states of mind might we be expected to see selected for?

The likelihood of persisting satisfaction seems quite small. Far more likely, we expect an abiding drive to reproduce, maybe some reward function when this behaviour is being pursued, but a lasting unsatisfactoriness when it is not.
